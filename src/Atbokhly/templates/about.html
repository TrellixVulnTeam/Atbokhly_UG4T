About our system

If normal user

Our site is a system that helps users to find out new meals and learn how to cook it. It provides the users with more than search method to find his desired meal using the meal ingredients or its name. Also the user can rate, like, comment on meals,view his liked meals,view the top searched meals over the entire site,or view his search history. If there is any complains he you contact us through our contact page. You can send a meal feedback to the chief.

If you are a chief

you can add,delete,or update meals. You can also do some functionality of the normal user like search,view meal,and view/edit your account settings. You can see the feedback on every meal and its details.
